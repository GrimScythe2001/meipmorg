Crippled Divinity
- Miegrat Sammri


Tell me, how do I escape?
I feel it's not only my fate.
I known I am the centre of all consciousness.

Yet I bear no memory of my becoming.
I know not how I came to walk amongst the living.
I've been stripped of my powers, I am condemned to atonement.

Crippled I've been, I cannot prove it just yet,
Given how insignificant my presence has now meant.
But every fibre of my being yearns for the grandeur I once had.

My body remembers what I cannot reclaim,
A heavy burden on my mind, shunned and shamed.
It's a curse, I fear, that I have placed upon meself.

Eons of longing, a sense of belonging.
The world I theatred- no gods, no miscreants.
Naivety got the best of me, and I cast meself to play a mere human.
