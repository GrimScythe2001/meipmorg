Maxim
- Miegrat Sammri

Just some haphazard thoughts. Not a chain/progression of ideas.


Self-doubt
Forgo my fate.
I am not destined for anything great.

Persevere
This too shall pass.
Time heals by eventually; nothing ever lasts.

Ephemeral
My body, my temple,
Rough-hewn from Earth- my motive scattered in dust.

Apprehension
When do I stumble, where do I fall?
It’s too good to be true; naught ever fares this fair.

Spell failure
He who plunges up retires to ground.
Lighter the feather dawns; heavier digs the crown.

Relentlessness
Girt bones to ashes.
Linger in comfort- repent forever.

Impetuous
Turn tail uninvited harbingers.
Don’t count the haystacks whilst the coffin’s still in production.

Consolation
Gone with the wind, the tiny whisper of dream.
It’s all noise- distorted untrue, lost forever in the undo.

Steadfast
What say my nay-sayers?
All in favour, give the proximal precipitious cliff a good tumble.

Cynicism
Life splashes you free lemonade, surprises overdue.
Ill omens begone; in lieu the advent, my prudent ordeal's here.
