Dear future me,
Are you doing well? Even though I'm writing this letter: I don't really know what kind of person you are. Are you having fun every day? Have you fallen in love yet? Did your dream of becoming a designer come true? I hope you've experienced many things and became a wonderful adult, better than I am right now.
- The younger you

Dear younger me,
I couldn't become a designer. I have fallen in love multiple times, but they were all short-lived. My job has been awful, I get yelled at every day. From time to time, I don't even know why I'm living anymore. I was weaker, more unfair, and better at deceiving myself than I thought I was. I couldn't become the wonderful adult you wanted, I'm sorry. I couldn't make our dream come true, and again, I'm sorry.
